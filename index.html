Step 2 Sign the Charter

Please read the BoardCompanions Charter carefully and confirm your agreement with a digital signature.

BoardCompanions Charter

BoardCompanions are experienced executives and business professionals who make their governance skills and expertise available to the boards of social profit organisations (SPOs), such as not-for-profit associations, foundations and social enterprises.

BoardCompanions Mission

  • Contribute to purpose and performance of SPO boards
  • Provide business experience to SPO boards, for societal benefit
  • Enrich own governance expertise through SPO exposure

BoardCompanions Values

  • High personal ethical and behavioural standards
  • Loyalty towards the SPOs we serve
  • Pursuit of positive and lasting impact

1. Experience and competencies

  • As a senior executive/business professional I am willing to contribute my diversified knowledge, experience and competencies to the SPO
  • I will seek impact by mutual learning from sharing experiences with my peers

2. Independency

  • I will take the interests of all stakeholders into consideration
  • I will not take on any activity which could be undermining my independence

3. Commitment and motivation

  • I will respect the purpose and culture of the SPO, and loyally serve its best interests
  • I will comply with all governance rules, regulations and legislations applicable to the SPO I serve
  • I am able and willing to provide time and flexibility to properly fulfil my role as Companion with the SPO

4. Integrity

  • I pursue high standards in terms of personal ethics, confidentiality and intellectual honesty
  • I will avoid any (appearance of) conflict of interest between the interest of the SPO and my personal interest

5. Membership of BoardCompanions

  • I believe that BoardCompanions can achieve more impact and personal growth through learning, mutual interaction and coaching
  • As a Companion, I will participate in learning activities and contribute to the collective intelligence of BoardCompanions
Date:
I have read the Charter and agree to its contents. I understand that my digital signature is legally valid.
